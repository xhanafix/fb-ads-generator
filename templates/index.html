ℹ️ Formula yang digunakan:

  • AIDA: Attention (Perhatian), Interest (Minat), Desire (Keinginan), Action (Tindakan)
  • PAS: Problem (Masalah), Agitation (Kegelisahan), Solution (Penyelesaian)
  • FAB: Features (Ciri), Advantages (Kelebihan), Benefits (Faedah)
  • 4U: Useful (Berguna), Urgent (Segera), Unique (Unik), Ultra-specific (Sangat Spesifik)
  • PASTOR: Problem, Amplify, Story, Testimony, Offer, Response
  • QUEST: Qualify, Understand, Educate, Stimulate, Transition
